04 / principles

Engineering Principles

Engineering decisions matter more than implementation details.

Software becomes easier to scale when responsibilities are clearly separated, systems are observable, and failures are expected rather than ignored.

The projects I build prioritize modular architecture, asynchronous communication, operational visibility, and maintainable system boundaries over short-term implementation speed.

Modularity

Independent components with clear responsibilities.

Observability

Logs, metrics, and tracing should make failures understandable.

Reliability

Recovery mechanisms are as important as successful execution.

Scalability

Design systems that continue to perform as traffic and complexity grow.
