04 / principles

Engineering principles

Software should be modular, observable, and resilient by design.

I believe well-designed systems make it easier for developers to reason about, extend, and operate software in production. Whether I am building AI agents, deployment platforms, or distributed services, I optimize for maintainability, reliability, and clear system boundaries.

I enjoy designing infrastructure that enables other developers to build faster and with confidence — platforms where complexity is handled at the infrastructure layer so that product teams can focus on what matters.

Modularity

Small, composable components age better than tightly coupled architectures.

Observability

Systems you cannot observe, you cannot improve. Instrument early.

Resilience

Design for failure. Recovery paths matter as much as happy paths.
